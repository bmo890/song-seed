# SongNook Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 11, 2026

SongNook is a songwriting app built around a simple promise: **your music stays yours, on your device.**

## What SongNook stores

Everything you create in SongNook — recordings, song ideas, lyrics, chords, notes, practice markers, and settings — is stored **locally on your device**. SongNook has no accounts, no sign-in, and no cloud service of its own. We cannot see, access, or recover your work, because it never reaches us.

## Backups and exports

When you back up, export, or share from SongNook, the resulting files go **only where you send them** — for example the Files app, iCloud Drive, Google Drive, or another app you choose through your device's share sheet. Those destinations are governed by their own privacy policies. SongNook never uploads anything automatically.

## What SongNook sends over the internet

SongNook makes a small number of anonymous network requests to provide specific features:

- **Word lookups** (rhymes, synonyms, related words) are sent to the Datamuse API when you use the Word Finder. Only the word you look up is transmitted — never your lyrics, recordings, or any identifier.
- **Public-domain book text** is fetched from Project Gutenberg / Gutendex when you use the Magpie lyric tool.

These requests contain no account information, no device identifiers we create, and nothing from your library. If you never use these features, SongNook makes no network requests at all.

## What SongNook collects

**Nothing.** SongNook contains no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no trackers. We do not collect usage data, personal information, or telemetry of any kind.

If the app crashes, a diagnostic log is written **locally on your device only**. It is never transmitted automatically — you can choose to share it with us from Settings → About when reporting a problem, and you can see exactly what it contains before sending it.

## Permissions

- **Microphone** — required to record your song ideas. Audio is captured only when you start a recording and is stored only on your device.
- **Notifications (Android)** — used to show the recording notification that lets recording continue in the background.

## Children's privacy

SongNook does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date. Because SongNook collects nothing, changes would only ever describe new optional features.
